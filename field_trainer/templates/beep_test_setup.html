Course Setup

Equipment Needed:

  • Cones or markers for two parallel lines
  • Measuring tape (15m or 20m)
  • Flat, non-slip surface

Setup Instructions:

  1. Measure and mark two parallel lines (15m or 20m apart)
  2. Place devices/cones evenly along each line
  3. Athletes start behind the START line
  4. Athletes run to the FINISH line at each beep
  5. At least one foot must cross the line before next beep
About Beep Test

Léger Protocol

  • 21 levels total
  • Starts at 8.5 km/h
  • Increases by 0.5 km/h per level
  • Each level ~60 seconds

How it works:

Athletes run between two lines at progressively faster speeds. Click FAIL when an athlete can't keep pace.
